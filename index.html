About the Waltham Rotary Club

The Waltham Rotary Club has proudly served the Waltham community for over 90 years. We promote service to others, support Waltham nonprofits, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders. We are a non-political and non-religious organization open to all.

Learn More

Who We Are

An international service organization who brings together business and professional leaders to provide humanitarian service and to advance goodwill and peace around the world.

Problems We Solve

From literacy and peace to water and health, we are always working to better our world, and we stay committed to the end.
